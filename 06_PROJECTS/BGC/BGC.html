BGC Water Supply and Sanitary Sewer Design


The design and construction of the building named BGC is planned in the center of Prishtina.
The building has 6 floors and two basements, with a total area of approximately 5500 m².
The water supply network system is the TE-CE system.
The building’s sewage system is planned to be implemented with PP pipes (for vertical lines and horizontal pipes in the basements) and PVC pipes for the bathroom and kitchen floors.
