Design of road "Tahir Zajmi"

The road is located in the center of the municipality of Gjakova.
It has a length of L = 357.50 m, traffic lane widths of 3.00 m, and parallel parking lanes on both sides with a width of B = 2.20 m.
There are sidewalks on both sides of the road, and between the traffic lanes there is a green park with a width of B = 11.00 m, which is used as a play area for children.
