Llukac's river flood modelling


Taking into account the recent floods occurring in several areas of Kosovo, I have developed a hydraulic model for a river segment (unnamed), with the purpose of analyzing flood‑prone zones under different scenarios of maximum discharges.
Basic river modelling data:

  • Length of the river segment: L = 1296.73 m
  • Cross sections every 30.00 m
  • Width of cross sections for modeling: 650 m on both sides of the river axis
  • Riverbed profile is mainly trapezoidal with dimensions B1 = 12 m, B2 = 19.50 m, and depth H = 2.50 m
  • Longitudinal slope of the river: 0.70%
  • Catchment basin area: S = 262.28 ha
