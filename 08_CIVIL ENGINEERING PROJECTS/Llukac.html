Llukac River

It is located in the northern part of the municipality of Gjakova.

  • The designed riverbed has a length of L = 1296.73 m;
  • The width of the riverbed is B = 14.00 m, and the height is H = 2.50 m;
  • The cross-section of the river is trapezoidal, with vegetated slopes and large stones that serve as protection against erosion;
  • The riverbed bottom remains in its natural state, allowing the continuation of aquatic life.
