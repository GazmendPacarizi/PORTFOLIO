Erenik River Basin Hydraulic Model using HEC-RAS

This hydraulic model represents a river basin with a surface area of 34.68 hectares, developed using HEC-RAS to simulate channel flow and floodplain dynamics. The model incorporates three distinct flow scenarios:

  • Base flow: 50 m³/
  • Design flow: 100 m³/
  • Historical maximum flow: 542 m³/s

Each scenario was analyzed to evaluate water surface profiles, inundation extents, and hydraulic performance under varying discharge conditions. The terrain was processed to reflect accurate cross-sections and flow paths, enabling reliable flood risk assessment and infrastructure planning. The historical peak flow provides a benchmark for extreme event simulation and resilience evaluation.
