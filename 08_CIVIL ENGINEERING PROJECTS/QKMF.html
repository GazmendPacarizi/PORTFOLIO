The Family Medicine Center Parking lot of Gjakova

The parking lot at the Family Medicine Center in Gjakova is located in the southern part of the Municipality of Gjakova.

  • Parking lot has an area of 3,900 m²;
  • The road for vehicle circulation within the parking lot is paved with natural stones, while the parking spaces are asphalted;
  • The entire area is equipped with a stormwater drainage system, which directs water runoff into the Krena River, which passes nearby;
  • The space around the Family Medicine Center is designed with sidewalks for pedestrian movement and green areas, enhancing the overall landscape;
  • This project was completed in 2023.
