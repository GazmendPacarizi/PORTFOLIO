Water supply system for Moglica neighborhood

The Green Neighborhood in the village of Moglicë is located in the southeastern part of the municipality of Gjakova.
The water supply network for this neighborhood has been designed.
Water supply will be provided from the city network.
The neighborhood consists of 85 residential houses and a commercial building at its entrance.

  • The water supply network has a total length of L = 891.00 m;
  • The used pipes for this network are HDPE PN 16 bar with diameters of Ø40 mm, Ø63 mm, Ø90 mm, and Ø110 mm;
  • The pipes designed for household connections are HDPE PN 16 bar Ø32 mm;
  • The connection of houses to the water supply network is planned to be done using clamp saddles;
  • For fire protection, 7 above-ground fire hydrants have been planned.
