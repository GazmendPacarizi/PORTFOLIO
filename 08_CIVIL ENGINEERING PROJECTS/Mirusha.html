Mirusha's river conceptual design


The conceptual design of the river segment in Malisheva envisions a harmonious integration between the natural flow of the riverbed and the urban fabric of the city. The riverbanks are reshaped to create gentle terraces that transition seamlessly into pedestrian walkways, encouraging both leisure and mobility.

The overall vision is to transform the river into a central urban feature—an ecological corridor that enhances biodiversity, improves water management, and strengthens the city’s connection to its natural landscape.
