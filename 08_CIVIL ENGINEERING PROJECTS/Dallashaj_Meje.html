Sanitary sewer system for villages from Dallashaj to Mejë

The sanitary sewer network for the villages of Dallashaj, Rracaj, Ramoc, Rrypaj, Madanaj, Jahoc, and Mejë has been designed with the purpose of diverting wastewater away from the river, where all residents currently discharge their sewage—either individually or through privately installed collectors from a neighborhood or group of houses.
The sanitary sewer network consists of four main collectors, which gather the wastewater through 47 branches.
The total length of the sanitary sewer system is Ltot = 32 + 568.74 km.
