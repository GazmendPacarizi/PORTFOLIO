Sanitary sewer system of Rrezina, Gjakovë

Rrezina is located in the northern part of the municipality of Gjakova.
It covers an area of approximately 92 hectares, which is planned for urbanization.
The sanitary sewage network for this neighborhood extends through paved roads and includes the following data:

  • The total length of the system is Ltot = 9708.27 m;
  • The sewage system consists of 5 main collectors and 33 branches, which will discharge into the Llukac River’s sanitary sewage network;
  • The diameters of all pipes are sufficient to handle the wastewater from future residential developments, considering that this part of Rrezinë is not yet urbanized;
  • The pipes used in this project are PE SN8 Ø250 mm, SN8 Ø315 mm, and SN8 Ø400 mm;
  • To connect future buildings, auxiliary sanitary sewer manholes with a diameter of D=250 mm are planned;
  • The sanitary sewer manholes are made of reinforced concrete, with a diameter of Ø1000 mm and heights varying according to the longitudinal profiles of the project;
  • The analysis and study of settlement needs were conducted for a 25-year period (T=25 years);
  • This project was completed in 2022.
