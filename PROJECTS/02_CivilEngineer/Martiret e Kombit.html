Stormwater sewer system of the road "Martirët e kombit"

The street “Martirët e kombit” extends in the southwestern part of the city of Gjakova. The width of the street is 7.00 meters, with sidewalks 3.00 meters wide on both sides of the street. The stormwater drainage system consists of two collectors.
The lengths of the collectors are L1 = 0+723.94 km and L2 = 0+497.35 km. The total length of the stormwater drainage system is Ltot = 1+246.69 km. The system uses HDPE SN8 pipes with the necessary diameters: OD 250mm, OD 315mm, and OD 400mm. Rainwater will be drained through the catch basins along the street.

This project has been designed in March, 2023.
